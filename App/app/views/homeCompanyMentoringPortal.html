Our Employee Internationalisation Programme is a new initiative and the first of its kind in the world. It is an inter-company initiative managed byOil Diversity Global encouraging companies to multi train and Internationalise their personnel in order to fast track them through their organisation and gain invaluable International experience. This not only encourages multi-discipline training but encourages companies to work in business collaboration together. This will all be facilitated and candidates matched with companies based on our clients requirements and end goal. We will follow these journeys and promote through the homepage of our website as well as International press when the initiative launches in July 2019.
