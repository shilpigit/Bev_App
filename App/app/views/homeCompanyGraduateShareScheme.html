GRADUATE SHARE SCHEME


Welcome to the Oil Diversity Graduate Scheme, this is an initiative to fast track graduates and to make sure we retain them within the Oil and Gas industry. The key to this is the business co-operation of our affiliated partners.
The idea is that by registering your company’s details below you agree to be contacted by affiliated companies to effectively swap graduates for a time period; this could be 2 weeks or 6 months with the idea to give the graduate more rounded and/or global experience.
It may be that you are having to let graduates go for a short period of time and an affiliated partner can use them within their organisation?
The scheme will be heavily advertised throughout local media, our quarterly magazine and website to promote the flexibility of the Oil and Gas industry in training its personnel. This is another route to help us to attract graduates into the Oil and Gas industry.
This feature will launch shortly, we will keep you informed.
